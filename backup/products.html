Our Products

Explore our comprehensive range of premium glass solutions

Float Glass

High-quality clear glass manufactured with precision for superior optical clarity and uniform thickness. The foundation of most architectural glass products.

  • Superior optical clarity
  • Uniform thickness
  • Available in various tints
  • Base for further processing

Tempered Glass

Heat-treated safety glass with exceptional strength and thermal resistance. Up to 5 times stronger than standard glass, it crumbles into small granular chunks when broken, reducing injury risk.

  • 5x stronger than annealed glass
  • Enhanced thermal resistance
  • Safety breakage pattern
  • Ideal for doors, showers, and facades

Laminated Glass

Two or more layers of glass bonded together with an interlayer (PVB/SGP). If broken, the glass is held in place by the interlayer, providing superior safety and security.

  • Safety and security
  • Sound insulation
  • UV protection (99%)
  • Design versatility

Insulated Glass

Multi-pane glass units separated by a spacer and sealed to create an insulating air/gas space. Essential for modern energy-efficient buildings.

  • Energy efficiency
  • Thermal insulation
  • Noise reduction
  • Condensation control

Decorative Glass

Artistic glass solutions including patterned, acid-etched, and ceramic printed glass. Perfect for adding privacy and aesthetic appeal to any space.

  • Custom patterns and designs
  • Privacy control
  • Light diffusion
  • Interior and exterior use
