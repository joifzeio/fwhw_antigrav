Mirror Solutions

Premium quality mirrors for architectural and decorative applications

Silver Mirror

Classic high-quality silver mirrors produced using advanced coating technology. Offers superior reflection, durability, and resistance to corrosion.

  • High reflection rate (>90%)
  • Copper-free and lead-free options
  • Excellent durability
  • Available in various thicknesses

Aluminum Mirror

Cost-effective mirror solution produced through vacuum magnetron sputtering. Ideal for furniture, cosmetic mirrors, and general applications.

  • Economical choice
  • Good reflection quality
  • Double coated paint protection
  • Wide range of applications

Antique Mirror

Decorative mirrors with a vintage aged appearance. Perfect for feature walls, furniture inlays, and creating unique interior atmospheres.

Safety Mirror

Mirrors backed with a special safety film (CAT I or CAT II) to prevent injury in case of breakage. Essential for gyms, dance studios, and public spaces.

  • Vinyl back safety film
  • Prevents shattering
  • High safety standard
  • Ideal for public areas
